MHA CCG Custom Character Card Generator

December 29, 2022 by Jorge Zhang

INSTRUCTIONS: In order, input the following:
  1. Hand Size: Choose a number.
  2. Symbols (x3): Choose the symbols.
  3. Character Name: Enter some text.
  4. Health: Value between 0 and 99.
  5. Ability: Enter text.
  6. Image: Enter a URL to an image, then click "INSERT IMAGE." Example: https://i.imgur.com/6ECF3c7.jpeg
After you are done, click the button. Screenshot that card, then once you have enough cards print them in a 9-card layout.
