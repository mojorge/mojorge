Official Rulebook

January 10, 2019December 15, 2018 by Jorge Zhang

Hey everyone! Over the past few days I have been writing up an official rulebook for Daggers High. I ended up choosing to use LaTex to make the rulebook because it’s simple and clean, and doesn’t require too much graphical design work. Plus, I already knew how to use LaTex (who knew it would be so useful)?

Anyway, you can find it here: 

Daggers High Rulebook V3.4Daggers High Rulebook

Please take a look and give me your thoughts and edits! If you see any spelling errors or things that don’t make sense I would be very grateful. Thanks for reading!

UPDATE: Thank you all for your edits and suggestions! I have incorporated most of them into the newest version of the rulebook, which I have now re-uploaded as Daggers High Rulebook V3.4. For now, this is the finalized version of the rulebook. That being said, please let me know if you have any other suggestions or feedback on how to make things clearer and better! Thanks :).
