3D Printed Heads

August 13, 2018 by Jorge Zhang

Hey everyone! I talked about Blender in a previous post to my blog (link below), and so I just wanted to showcase something cool you can do with that program: draw and 3D print heads.

Each of these heads took about 12 hours to make, but I did them from scratch instead of importing previous designs. That is part of the reason why there are only two of them: they just take too long to make! You can notice a pretty large improvement from the first design to the second as I got more comfortable using Blender. Still, both heads are supposed to look like me so in that sense I kind of failed. Let me know what you think in the comments below, and make sure to subscribe if you enjoy this content!
