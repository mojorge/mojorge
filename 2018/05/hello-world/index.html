First post!

May 11, 2018

Hello everyone, and welcome to my blog! My name is Jorge Zhang and I am a student and board game designer. Right now I am working with a friend on developing my first board game, Dagers High, and am taking a gap year before college to publish it. We are pretty far in the process, and now are working on incorporating some feedback from play tests into the game. You can see some photos here!

I wanted to start blogging to document my process as a designer, as well as potentially post some other fun things like puzzles or some thoughts on things. I’m not completely sure yet.

Anyway, feel free to leave comments or let me know how I am doing on any of my blog posts by reaching out to gomission2009@gmail.com. Thanks! See you all later!
