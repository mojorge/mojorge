How this works

  1. We will receive your mailing address from this form.
  2. Using this information, we will send you a payment request for $1.99 via Zelle to your email address. If you prefer, you can send the payment directly to jorgezhang17@gmail.com. If the game was already paid for via PayPal, we will not send a Zelle request.
    • Using Zelle helps us reduce payment processing fees (generally 2.9% + $.30 or higher for services like Paypal). You will not be able to pay with a credit card, as Zelle only does bank to bank transfers.
    • We manually process these, so please expect up to a 2 business day delay and keep an eye out in your inbox.
    • If, for whatever reason, we cannot send you a game, we will email you back to inform you of this.
    • We can accept other payment processing services, but the customer will be responsible for an additional fee. If you would like to use a service other than PayPal or Zelle, please note this in the "anything else" box and we will reach out to you with further steps.
  3. Once the invoice is paid, we will send you the game in the mail. The game will be sent in an envelope. US only.
  4. The cards are produced on high quality linen paper and a matte finish, but there is also an alternative lamination option. If you would prefer lamination, please note this in the "anything else" box. Laminated cards last longer and are a better option if you do not mind the appearance.
