Blue Giant previews Daggers High

March 9, 2019 by Jorge Zhang

Hey everyone! Today, I’d like to share with you a video that Blue Giant Media recently released. They made a gameplay preview of Daggers High that went through Freshman year, and talked about setting up the game. Check out the video below:

It’s really neat to see your game be played by someone else. I got a lot of great feedback from Michael as well, and I really appreciate the amount of time and effort he volunteered to help create this video. Having taken an attempt at making gameplay videos myself, it is very difficult and can be unrewarding work at times. It’s also very difficult to avoid small mistakes, especially when playing someone else’s game with admittedly complex gameplay and just a rulebook in hand. Blue Giant Media did an amazing job with this. So thank you, Blue Giant Media!
