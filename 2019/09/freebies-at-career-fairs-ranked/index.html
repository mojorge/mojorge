Freebies at Career Fairs Ranked

September 29, 2019 by Jorge Zhang

Companies: take notes.

A couple of days ago, my school hosted a career fair with some of the most prestigious companies from around North America. While there, I made sure to pick up a bunch of freebies. Here’s what I picked up:

Phone Stand by AlphaSights

Score: 0/5

I was pretty excited when I picked up this free phone stand- unfortunately, this one was pretty low quality and could barely support my phone’s weight. It also broke after a few hours.

Fidget Objects by Savills and Market Axess

Score: 0/5

I’m not quite sure what compelled me to pick these up, but these are very low quality fidget objects. I should probably dispose of them after this review.

Earphones by EY

Score: 1/5

I was super excited to pick up these earphones- and I’d have rated them higher if they weren’t possibly the worst quality earphones I’ve ever used before.

Highlighter+sticky notes by The Public Defender Service

Score: 2/5

The highlighter is actually quite nice, though the sticky notes are awkward sizes.

Chapstick by BD

Score: 3/5

Free chapstick is usually pretty crappy, but I was pleasantly surprised by how well this one has been holding up.

Free Folders and Notebooks

Score: 3/5

These aren’t of the highest quality, but it certainly beats buying them at the overpriced campus store.

Reusable Straw by Heidrick & Struggles

Score: 3/5

I can’t deny that this is a pretty sweet metal straw… on the other hand, will I ever actually use this? Rated a 3 for the pipe-cleaner that comes with.

Anti-Zuckerburg Device by Redfive

Score: 3/5

This was probably the most common freebie I saw at the career fair. Probably because it’s just two pieces of thin plastic- that being said, I can’t deny that it works pretty well.

Portable Measuring Tape by NIST

Score: 3.5/5

This is actually a pretty sweet measuring tape that measures up to 60in. and also automatically retracts after pressing a button.

A book about being happy after 50 by the CEO of AARP

Score: ???/5

I would have rated this, but I just couldn’t get through the first few pages.

And there you have it: all the freebies at the career fair ranked! What’s the best thing you’ve ever gotten for free? Let me know in the comments below, and thanks for reading!
