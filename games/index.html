Games

Check out some of these video games that I programmed! If you are looking for tabletop games I designed, you can find those on the homepage.


3 Triangles

This is a print and play puzzle game designed by UberDante that I adapted into a digital form.


Highschool Simulator

This text-based adventure is based on Daggers High, a board game I designed. It takes the player through their four years of high school, in which the player attempts to obtain as many resume points as possible. These can be obtained through various means, including but not limited to: getting A's, leading clubs, and getting teacher rec letters.


The sky is falling

This simple mining game features a blue cube trying to escape a falling sky.
